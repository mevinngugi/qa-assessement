## Session Chatter 2
**Charter:** Exploring Valr's sample trading page to understand how the trading process works and find potential bugs.  
**Timeboxed:** Wednesday, 10:52 -  11:16 am.  
**Areas:** — https://www.valr.com/trade/BTCZAR 
**Observations:**  The url prodived in the assessment did not work. I raised it with the Valr team and they provided a working url - https://www.valr.com/en/exchange/BTC/ZAR and made the following observations:
- It's a Bitcon to South African Rand trading pair.
- If the user is not logged in, they may only view the data but not trade. If they try to trade, they will be prompted to log in or sign up.
- On the order form, the user can not pass decimal values for the price.
- price * amount(BTC) = total(ZAR). If the user enters a price and amount, the total is calculated correctly. 
- Interestingly, if the user keys in a price of 1 and a total(ZAR) of 10 the the Amount (BTC) of 10 is populated. I am not sure if this is correct but something worth investigating further.
